Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Daan Setu.

How quickly can food donations be picked up?
We typically arrange pickups within 2-4 hours of a donation request, depending on location and availability of our partner NGOs.
What types of food can be donated?
We accept cooked meals, packaged foods, fresh produce, and baked goods. All donations must be fresh, properly stored, and safe for consumption.
Is there any cost for using Daan Setu?
No, our platform is completely free for both donors and NGOs. We're supported by grants and partnerships to keep this service accessible.
