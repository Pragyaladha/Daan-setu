Our Story

Daan Setu was born from a simple observation: while millions go hungry in India, tons of perfectly good food are wasted every day from restaurants, events, and households.

Founded in 2023, we started as a small initiative to connect local restaurants with NGOs. Today, we're India's fastest-growing food donation network, facilitating thousands of meals daily across major cities.

Our platform leverages technology to make food donation as simple as ordering food online. With real-time matching, logistics support, and impact tracking, we ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it most, quickly and safely.
